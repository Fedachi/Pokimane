Who Is Pokimane?

Who am I?

I am known as Pokimane, a youtuber and twitch streamer that plays many genres of games. I have also played with many other big youtubers such as Jacksepticeye, Pewdiepie and Markiplier. Other than that I am also a part of Offline TV, a group of friends that are all content creators just like myself.

What do I do?

As a youtuber I record and edit entertaining videos for my viewers to watch. They comment what they thought and like if they want to see more of it. As a twitch streamer I go live on twitch everyday to play games live and to talk to my viewers.

On youtube I have editors that help me edit my videos while on twitch I have my mods that help regulate and make sure that everything is under control while I'm streaming.
