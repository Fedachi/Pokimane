Chat Ranks

Every minute that you're messaging, you randomly gain between 15 and 25 XP. To avoid spamming, earning XP is limited to once a minute per user. In the server, you type !rank to see your rank and level.

You unlock the whole server once you hit level 5 on mee6. Reaching Level 5 takes between 46 messages to 77 messages, on average 58 messages / about an hour of chatting.

Any violations of the rules with result in a RESET of your rank.
